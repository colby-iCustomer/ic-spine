# Audience Loop — Positioning

> Single doc, website-readable. Pillars → personas → key use cases. Owner: Colby.

## What ALoop is

Self-serve, browser-based growth-marketing system for SMB. Compounds on first-party audience interest. Always-on, decision-driven.

> Three pillars from the brand level, expressed in ALoop's voice. To be locked.

## Personas

- **Growth marketer** — runs SMB campaigns, owns acquisition + nurture
- **GTM operator** — RevOps style; instruments the funnel
- **Founder / solo PM** — owns the whole growth motion at a small company

## Key use cases

> Top-of-funnel + middle-of-funnel use cases ALoop is designed for.

- **ABM in a box** — ICP definition + matched audiences + LinkedIn / Reddit / Meta activation
- **Always-on event audiences** — scan Luma / Partiful → push to LinkedIn or email sequence
- **CRM retargeting** — HubSpot contacts → LinkedIn / Meta retargeting with decision-trace attribution
- **Audience-led campaign creation** — chat-driven campaign setup, multi-channel
- **Outcomes loop** — UTM + CAPI + decision trace closes the attribution loop

## What ALoop is not

- Not a CDP
- Not a campaign management tool (we orchestrate, customer owns the seat)
- Not an enterprise data warehouse (that's where we read from)
