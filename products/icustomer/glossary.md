# iCustomer — Brand glossary

> Cross-product terms. For platform-structure terms, see [architecture/reference.md](../../architecture/reference.md). For product-specific terms, see each product's glossary.

| Term | Definition |
|---|---|
| **iCustomer** | The company / brand. Three product tiers: Audience Loop (SMB), Decision OS (Enterprise), OneSource (Developer). |
| **Decision Context Layer** | iCustomer's positioning — orchestration middle between data and channels |
| **iAgent** | Customer-facing product agent (distinct from internal coding agents) |
| **iWorker** | Same as iAgent — role-based digital twin |
| **Decision Trace** | Audit trail of every decision |
